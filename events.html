Main Events

Blitzkrieg

Ready to set the stage on fire? The dream of every college-based rock band, the ultimate musical fiesta, Blitzkrieg is here again! With volumes soaring sky high and passions even higher, Blitzkrieg has a history of wreaking havoc and this year it is set to break all past records!. The Best of Bands will be shortlisted to feature in these eliminations and will battle it out during the prelims and then the finalists will proceed for the showdown at one of our pro-nights with their performances being judged by the yet to be revealed Headlining Performance. The Winning Band will take away the Extraordinary prize, along with prizes for different subcategories.

Voice Outs

Music, a medium of expressions, which knows no bounds no definitions, never fails in reviving one's moods. Euphoria brings to you an unprecedented experience - "Voice Outs", the singing competition. Grab the stage, move the audience with one single tone and make the crowd forget themselves for your performance. Behind the inscrutable chords and melodies, just be your best self and own the stage.

Come, grab it

DJ Wars

Calling all Djs,the hunt for crazy musical geniuses is on!Show your talent and watch the crowd go wild on the dance floor.Mix and match the songs and create magic.Light up the night with your awesome DJing skills and get grooving.Get the party started!

Fresh Paint

Do you have the passion for art? We welcome you to let your imagination flow free and to let the world know the charm of your art as you exhibit it through "Fresh Paint". Fresh Paint is dedicated to nurturing the beauty of art that manifests the individuality of participants. The desire to inspire and foster the talents is contented through it.

Lights Camera Action!

Short films are as elegant, expressive and creative as feature-length films. They embrace writers as prominent as other cinematic storytelling. "Lights Camera Action!" celebrates admiring the beauty of Short films. The Short Film Contest challenges the participant's creativity and imagination and inspires them to combine their ideas with our theme.
